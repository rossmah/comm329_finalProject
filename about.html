About Me

Hello, I'm a Software Engineer from Upstate New York. I am currently pursuing a bachelors degree of Computer Science from Clarkson University with minors in Electrical Engineering, Information Technology, Mathematics, and Business. I have interned in technical roles at telecommunication and insurance companies, and I conduct undergraduate research regarding human-robot interaction on campus. Post-graduation, I will be working full time in an Engineering Leadership Program for a defense contracting company in New England.

I have a strong interest in problem-solving and blending creativity and technology together. I am particularly passionate about Software Engineering, Frontend Development, and UI/UX Designer positions. I am always looking for opportunities to gain experience in an ever-evolving field that I love!

Please explore this site to get a glimpse of my work and to learn more about myself.
